it's a simple game of blackjack: try to get an ace with a face card, or a pair of aces.


to spice it up, you can either split it (cut the deck in half), tip it (bribe the dealer: if you're lucky, the person will give you an extra ace. if not, he or she might remove an ace), or redeal it.
